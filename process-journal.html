Week 2 - Interaction Design & Robot City & Intelligent Transportation System

The industrial revolution mentioned in class really inspired me a lot especially in real-time synchronization and future 5G Robotic industry. It is not a conceptual idea where highly self-processed AI robots contribute in every sections of the city. With the newest 5G technology, the status (envrioment, weather, crowdness, etc...) can be uploaded and shared to each and every system in real time. Having AI robot to process most works are possible now. At current, human power are better than robot(AI) because of the flexibility in information processing. E.g. automobil can not handle emergent situation well. A storm may affect how automobile receiving light signal, it can not predict if something is rushing into it or not. However, if we have real-time information, automobiles will be better than human-ride cars as it process more information and count more possibilities than human-beings.

Considering the movie 《Sully》, the delay in the section where information is exchanged can cause a lot of trouble, so human power is needed to resolve these problems, if machine itself can process the info, it will be much more efficient.

Week 5 - Wireframing

Wireframe is very important in design UX(user experiences). As designers, we should be able to capture who is our potential user, what they need, in what way they want service be delivered. We also need to consider what we can do, what are the limitations, what will be proper.

An example i can think of is the radio back in China. I always wonder why digital devices like radio can be so hot among the elders. I figured it out as i look into the view of an elder. Radio does not have fancy functions as smart phone or computers. Hoever, old people who are retired are likely to spend a day walking or staying at home. A portable device that talks random and interesting events and can last for days will be extremly suitable for them.

Week 6 - Design Patterns

I used to think patterns are combinations of different shapes, color, and fonts. But i found that patterns are just a group of solutions that keeps the consistant without changing. It is important to keep the pattern in the UI. It is hard to keep users in track if the patterns are changing. Also solutions also matters as bad solution can confuse users and will lead to a bad experience

At the very basic, the solution is really important. If having a solution that some how violet the users needs, it will ends up being a fail when combined into patterns

Prototype is also the key to know if users' needs are satisfied or is there any unnecessary functions.

Week 11 - IDX Futures - Autonomy, Agency, Assurance

Autonomy-Being able to do things on its own.

Agency-Has a decision tactic.

Assurance-Being able to carry out tasks properly

E.g. Self-driving Car

Autonomy - being able to start engine, accelerate, break, stop by its own.

Agency - Has the ability to check and select which way to go. (fastest path, less traffic, etc...)

Assurance - self-driving cars will try different ways to approach to the destination.
