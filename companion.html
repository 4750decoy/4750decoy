1. Question Feature

Through research methods such as interviews with ski patrol and questionaires to those who have experienced lost in backcountry, we have put some questions that helps the rescue group to quickly identify the current situation and provide needed help as soon as possible.

In the feedback section, we have disccussed the necessity of having questions at the very start. Some argue that users in emergent situation might be very impatient to fill these questions. However, it is also a big concern that the ski patrol may not get details they need.

2. Phone Call

According to our findings in the first two assessment, it is clear to us that lost peoples' emotions need to be very stable and calm. In this way, they can be much more capable of chossing the correction response to whatever happened. Some people will be frightened or worried when they find they are lost. Contacting is the best way to keep them calm as they can feel they are helped and accompanied.

At first, we only considered contacting ski patrol and paramedic. It comes to our mind that contacting family or friends might e even better in helping users to control their emotion.

3. Medical

Over 100 cases we have researched, we have found that most people are not an expert in skiing or hiking. Most of them don't know what to do if injured. In order to help them out, medical pages are developed to help them quickly identify what type of injuries they have and get advise from the paramedic. There are also stress relief feature where breathing exercises are introduced to reduce users stress.

4. Recreation

Since drones need to stay with users utill ski patrol come to rescue, we thought it would be better that users can have some recreation functions to divert their attentions so that they can be less worried and less panic.

Figure 4.1 - Recreation Page-old

Expert William suggested that having a recreation function might be improper in such circumstances. As we disccussed, enviroment in backcountry is tough. There are so many unpredictable changes that may threat users lives. We do want users can be less panic, but we would not risk users' lives for that.

5. GPS

Understand the surrounding eviroment is also crucial. When our users are able to move, it is comparatively safer to stay in an open ground rather than cliffs or steep slopes. In addition, having a map where you can see the rescue approaching can make our uses feel being secured.

6. Color & Text

We have selected blue color as the background as it does not conflict with the nature (Blue Sky, Green Leef, White Snow). Colors such as red, pink, purple, and orange will make a strong conflict which irritate users and make them feel more nervous. However, strong colors are used with different option buttons to make it more apparent and easy to access.

The text is sans-serif which looks smooth. The text color is white that stand out in a blue back ground.
